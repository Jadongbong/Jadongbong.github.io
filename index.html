About Me

Hi, my name is Jaden He. I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, where I am studying Physics and Piano Peformance, with intention to add a third major in Mathematics. Currently, I am working as an undergraduate researcher with Nathan Schine in the Join Quantum Institute. My interests span multiple areas of math and physics, and I have yet to decide on a specialization. You can find out more about what I'm doing in the research section.

My passion for physics extends beyond the classroom, and I am committed to physics outreach and exposing as many people to physics as possible. I am currently a tutor for UMD's Society of Physics Students and a volunteer with UMD Physics Outreach.

In my free time, I love playing classical piano, lifting weights, leveling up my coding skills, and dabbling in sports. You can find out more about my hobbies in the hobbies section.
