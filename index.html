About Me

Hi, my name is Jaden He. I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, where I am studying Physics and Piano Peformance, with intention to add a third major in Mathematics. Currently, I am working as an undergraduate researcher with Nathan Schine in the Join Quantum Institute. My interests span multiple areas of math and physics, and I have yet to decide on a specialization.

My passion for physics extends beyond the classroom, and I am committed to physics outreach and exposing as many people to physics as possible.
